What we study

Our research focus areas

Our lab pursues questions at the intersection of clinical observation and translational science, with programs spanning four interconnected domains.

🫀

Cardiovascular Disease

Investigating novel biomarkers and interventional strategies for heart failure, arrhythmia, and coronary artery disease in diverse patient populations.

🧬

Precision Medicine

Leveraging genomic and proteomic data to tailor therapeutic regimens and predict individual patient response to treatment.

🧠

Neurological Outcomes

Characterizing cognitive trajectories in chronic illness and developing scalable screening tools for early neurological decline.

📊

Clinical Epidemiology

Applying rigorous observational methods and natural experiments to understand disease etiology and health system interventions at scale.
