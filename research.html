Our research

Active studies & programs

Our portfolio spans clinical trials, longitudinal cohort studies, and translational research. Below is a summary of current and recently completed work.

Clinical trials

Interventional studies

Cardiovascular

APEX-HEART II: Biomarker-Guided Diuresis in Acute Heart Failure

A randomized controlled trial evaluating whether serial NT-proBNP-guided diuresis improves 90-day outcomes in patients hospitalized with acute decompensated heart failure. Enrolling at 6 sites nationally.

NCT0000000 · Phase III · 6 sites · Target n=480

Recruiting
Precision Medicine

PREDICT-CV: Polygenic Risk Score Integration in Primary Prevention

Pilot RCT testing whether disclosing a validated cardiovascular polygenic risk score to patients and clinicians improves guideline-concordant statin prescribing over 12 months.

NCT0000001 · Phase II · 2 sites · Target n=200

Active
Cardiovascular

APEX-HEART I: Troponin Kinetics in Stable Outpatients

Completed single-arm study characterizing high-sensitivity troponin trajectories in a stable outpatient cardiovascular cohort. Results published in Circulation, 2023.

NCT0000002 · Phase II · n=312 enrolled

Cohort & observational studies

Longitudinal programs

Epidemiology

APEX Longitudinal Cohort (ALC)

An ongoing prospective cohort of 2,400 patients with established cardiovascular risk, with annual clinical assessments, biobank samples, and linked EHR data. Established 2016; follow-up through 2028.

Active
Neurology

Cognitive Aging in Cardiac Disease (CACD)

Cross-disciplinary study characterizing longitudinal cognitive trajectories in patients with heart failure using validated neuropsychological assessments, neuroimaging, and plasma biomarkers.

Recruiting
Health Equity

Disparities in Arrhythmia Care (DAC)

Retrospective cohort study using linked registry data to examine racial and socioeconomic disparities in atrial fibrillation detection, anticoagulation initiation, and ablation referral across 12 health systems.
