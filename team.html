Our lab draws together physicians, scientists, biostatisticians, and trainees united by a commitment to careful, consequential research.

Principal Investigator

MR
Dr. M. Reyes, MD, PhD
Professor & Lab Director

Dr. Reyes trained in internal medicine and cardiovascular disease before completing a research fellowship in clinical epidemiology. Her lab focuses on the intersection of biomarker science, precision therapeutics, and health equity. She holds an NIH R01 and an AHA Transformational Project Award, and has mentored over 30 trainees who have gone on to faculty positions.
